Book Exchange

The purpose of The Pingry Book Exchange is to improve the Pingry community by allowing students to easily buy and sell used schoolbooks from each other in a cost-efficient manner. It is to the discretion of the students to negotiate the price and decide upon the location of the exchange. This way, students have the flexibility to accommodate purchases to fit to their own schedules and locations.

Selling a Book

  • Select the department and course that your book falls under.
  • Click "add new listing."
  • Add the ISBN, condition, and a comment. An image of the book, the author(s), and title will automatically appear next to the listing to make it easy for the seller and buyer to confirm it's the right book. Once complete, click "save listing."

Buying a Book

  • Select the department and course of the book you are looking for.
  • Once you find a book, click on the name of the owner next to "E-mail" to contact them.

Picking up a Book

  • Once you find a book that you want, it is up to you and the owner to complete the rest of the purchase. By e-mail, or any other means that you like, you can agree upon a price for the book and a location (whether it be at Pingry or somewhere else) to meet to pick it up.

Deleting a Book

  • Click "Edit this book" located below your listed book
  • Under Ad Status, change the ad to "inactive" and then click "save listing."
